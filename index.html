About

E.B. Oliver Wolf writes horror for those who like their shadows alive. His stories drip with unease, built from the mundane moments we trust and twisted into something unforgettable. Raised on Hitchcock and The Twilight Zone, he crafts quick, unsettling tales with psychological bite—perfect for late nights when the floorboards creak, or when you swear you saw something move in the corner of your eye. Based in Pennsylvania, he writes for readers who crave the thrill of being haunted long after the last page.

Darker, longer collections are taking shape in the shadows. Until then, expect sharp shocks—stories meant to linger like whispers in an empty room.

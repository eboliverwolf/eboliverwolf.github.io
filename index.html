Sweet Little Riley

Short Psychological Horror

About the Story

"Sweet Little Riley" is a psychological horror short story that explores the chilling transformation of identity and the dark consequences of cruelty. Both intimate and terrifying, it challenges readers to question the true nature of beauty, identity, and the monsters within us.

About the Author

E.B. Oliver Wolf is a horror writer inspired by the eerie storytelling of Hitchcock, Serling, and classic cult horror films. Originally from Los Angeles, he now writes from the quieter pace of Pennsylvania. His work blends psychological depth, haunting imagery, and unsettling twists.
